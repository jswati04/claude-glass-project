Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our products and services.

  • What types of glass products do you offer?

    JGS Industries offers a comprehensive range of glass products including tempered glass, double glazed glass, laminated glass, colored laminated glass, glass with Georgian bars, fabric glass, switchable glass, fire-rated glass, and bullet-proof glass. Our products cater to various architectural, commercial, and residential applications.

  • Do you provide custom glass solutions?

    Yes, we specialize in custom glass solutions tailored to your specific requirements. Our team works closely with architects, designers, and contractors to create bespoke glass products that meet the unique needs of each project. Contact us with your specifications for a personalized consultation.

  • What is the lead time for glass orders?

    Lead times vary depending on the type and complexity of the glass product. Standard items typically take 1-2 weeks, while custom or specialty glass solutions may require 3-6 weeks. For large projects, we recommend contacting us early in the planning process to ensure timely delivery.

  • Do you offer installation services?

    Yes, we offer professional installation services for our glass products. Our experienced installation team ensures that all glass products are installed correctly and safely, meeting industry standards and building codes. We also work with a network of certified contractors across the region.

  • What warranties do you provide for your glass products?

    Our glass products come with warranties that vary by product type. Typically, we offer 5-10 year warranties against manufacturing defects. For specific warranty information on each product, please refer to the product documentation or contact our customer service team.
