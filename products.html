Tempered Glass (600x400)

Tempered Safety Glass

Our tempered glass is heat-treated to create a product that is 4-5 times stronger than annealed glass. When broken, it shatters into small granular pieces instead of sharp shards, significantly reducing the risk of injury.

Key Features:

  • Enhanced strength and safety
  • Heat resistant up to 250°C
  • Shatters into small granular pieces when broken
  • Meets international safety standards
  • Available in various thicknesses and sizes

Technical Specifications

Thickness 4mm - 19mm
Strength 4-5 times stronger than annealed glass
Heat Resistance Up to 250°C
Applications Window panes, doors, shower enclosures, furniture

Double Glazed Glass

Superior insulation and energy efficiency for modern buildings

Double Glazed Glass (600x400)

Double Glazed Insulating Units

Our double glazed units consist of two glass panes separated by a spacer and sealed to create an insulating air or gas-filled cavity. This design significantly improves thermal and acoustic insulation, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort.

Key Features:

  • Superior thermal insulation
  • Excellent sound reduction
  • Reduces condensation on windows
  • Available with argon or krypton gas fill
  • Compatible with various frame systems

Technical Specifications

Total Thickness 18mm - 36mm
U-Value 1.0 - 2.8 W/m²K (depending on configuration)
Sound Reduction Up to 35dB
Applications Windows, doors, curtain walls, facades
