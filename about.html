Our Export Capabilities

Packaging & Logistics

Our specialized glass packaging ensures safe transit across international borders. We use custom wooden crates with reinforced corners and shock-absorbing materials to protect our premium glass products during long-distance shipping.

Documentation & Compliance

Our experienced export team manages all documentation requirements including certificates of origin, packing lists, commercial invoices, and customs declarations. We ensure compliance with all international regulations and standards.

Shipping & Delivery

With established partnerships with international freight forwarders, we provide seamless delivery services from our facility to your project site. We offer air, sea, and land transportation options to meet your timeline and budget requirements.

Global Standards Compliance

Our glass products are certified to meet international quality and safety standards including ISO, ASTM, EN, and region-specific requirements. We provide all necessary certification documentation for hassle-free customs clearance.

Our Export Process

Initial Consultation

We discuss your project requirements, including technical specifications, quantity, timeline, and destination.

Quotation & Order

We provide a detailed quotation including product, shipping, and documentation costs. Upon approval, your order is confirmed.

Production & Quality Control

Your glass products are manufactured to your specifications and undergo rigorous quality checks to meet international standards.

Packaging & Documentation

Products are carefully packaged for international shipping, and all export documentation is prepared for smooth customs clearance.

Shipping & Tracking

Your order is shipped via the most appropriate method (air, sea, or land), and you receive regular updates on its progress.

Delivery & Support

We coordinate with local partners for customs clearance and final delivery, providing technical support throughout the installation process.
